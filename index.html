Tutorial:

This is a site to practice writing in your target language and producing easy to copy, easy to correct text.

Press "Tab" to toggle between the two text boxes.
Press "Enter" to translate text, and "Shift + Enter" to save your sentences below for easy copying.
You can also press "Shift + Backspace" to delete what you're currently typing.
